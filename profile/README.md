# Snow River Advisory

**Snow River Advisory** is a fractional CFO + Controller firm serving small and mid-sized **nonprofit organizations in Alaska**.

We are currently building the firm’s foundational systems and resources ahead of a planned public launch in **early 2027**. Our work is grounded in three principles:

- **Clarity** — Transforming complexity into financial understanding.  
- **Insight** — Turning data into strategic guidance for confident decision-making.  
- **Stewardship** — Honoring mission-driven work through disciplined and transparent financial practices.  

This GitHub organization hosts selected resources that reflect our approach to nonprofit financial management, including:

- Example financial models and forecasting templates  
- Sample reporting layouts and board-ready dashboards  
- Checklists, tools, and other materials we choose to share publicly
